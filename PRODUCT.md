# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Everyday consumers (18–45) who saw a viral ad or a friend's clip and want to make a funny, shareable video of a family member — typically their grandma — dancing a trending TikTok dance. They arrive on mobile, mid-scroll, with a photo in their camera roll and 30 seconds of attention. Zero tolerance for setup friction; the job is "photo in → hilarious video out → share it in the group chat."

## Product Purpose

DancingGrandma turns a single photo of a person plus a reference dance video (a TikTok dance) into a new AI-generated video where that person performs the dance, music included. Success = the user laughs out loud at the preview and hits share. The web app is the product: upload photo, pick or upload a dance, generate, share. (MVP: generation is mocked while the video-model research lands; the flow is real end-to-end.)

## Brand Personality

Playful, warm, a little absurd. Three words: **cheeky · festive · family-warm**. The humor is affectionate — we laugh *with* grandma, never at her. The vibe is a kitchen disco at a family party: confetti energy, big grins, someone's uncle filming vertically. English UI, meme-literate copy, but never mean or ironic-cold.

## Anti-references

- Sterile AI-tool SaaS: dark dashboard, purple gradients, "Powered by cutting-edge AI" badges, hero-metric rows.
- Corporate video-editor landing pages (generic feature grids, laptop mockups).
- Ironic/edgy meme sites that punch down — the affection is the brand.
- The 2026 cream-and-beige "warm AI" default, and editorial display-serif magazine aesthetics — this is a party, not a periodical.

## Design Principles

1. **The demo IS the pitch** — show a before/after (photo → dancing video) above the fold; don't explain what a paragraph can't sell but a clip can.
2. **Three taps to joy** — photo, dance, go. Every screen has exactly one obvious next action.
3. **Affectionate absurdity** — copy and motion are allowed to be silly; grandma is always the hero, never the punchline.
4. **Party, committed** — color and typography go big (committed/full-palette strategy); timid is off-brand.
5. **Made for the group chat** — every output state is one tap from share; vertical video is a first-class citizen.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Body text ≥4.5:1, large display text ≥3:1. Full `prefers-reduced-motion` alternatives for all confetti/dance motion. Upload flows keyboard-navigable with clear focus states; video previews always have controls and never autoplay with sound.
